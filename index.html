LORE

  • 1951/2 - Stan and Ford are born
  • 1964 (probably) - Stan and Ford find the Stan-O-War (they use a sharpie to write their names in the cave where the boat was and sharpies were first sold in 1964)
  • 1964/5 - Stan and Ford turn 13 and have a bar mitzvah (Stan’s bar mitzvah is mentioned in the journal)
  • 1968/69/70 - a baby is born, who may or may not be Shermie Pines
  • 1969/70 - Stan is kicked out and Ford goes to university
  • 1975 - Ford goes to Gravity Falls
  • 1978 - Ford finishes Journal 1 and starts Journal 2 (in the blacklight journal he says Journal 1 covers his first three years in Gravity Falls)
  • 1979 - Ford meets Bill
  • 1981 - Ford starts Journal 3
  • 1982 - Fiddleford goes down to help with the portal
  • 1983 - portal goes wrong
  • 1999 - Dipper and Mabel are born
  • 2012 - the entire show happens
